Job Title: Data Analyst

Responsibilities:

  • Collect and clean data from various sources.
  • Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns.
  • Prepare reports and visualizations to communicate insights.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support data-driven decision-making.
  • Stay up-to-date with data analysis tools and techniques.

Job Description:

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our dynamic team. The ideal candidate will have a passion for working with data and a strong analytical mindset. In this role, you will be responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data to provide valuable insights to our organization.

Responsibilities:

  • Collect and clean data from various sources.
  • Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns.
  • Prepare reports and visualizations to communicate insights.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to support data-driven decision-making.
  • Stay up-to-date with data analysis tools and techniques.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., Statistics, Data Science, Computer Science).
  • Proficiency in data analysis tools such as Python, R, or SQL.
  • Strong problem-solving and critical-thinking skills.
  • Excellent communication and data presentation abilities.
  • Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI) is a plus.

Location:

Rennes (35)

Employment Type:

Full-Time

How to Apply:

If you are a data enthusiast who enjoys turning data into actionable insights, we would love to hear from you! Please submit your resume and a cover letter outlining your relevant experience to email@example.com.
